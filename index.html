TECHNICAL APPROACH & LEGAL SYNTHESIS BREAKDOWN

Gordon Casebook represents the pinnacle of Adri Aufa's unique dual expertise as a Civil Law student and Software Architect. Designed specifically for law students and practicing attorneys in Common Law Jurisdictions, this Micro-SaaS automatically extracts critical case insights and constructs standardized IRAC Case Briefs (Issue, Rule, Analysis/Application, Conclusion) alongside structured Legal Risk Mitigation frameworks from pasted text or uploaded PDF court files up to 10MB.

Architected using the robust Laravel PHP framework for secure routing and document handling, normalized with a MySQL database for state management, and integrated directly with custom OpenAI API prompt pipelines to ensure high legal domain precision. Currently deployed on high-performance Fly.io cloud infrastructure (https://micro-saas.fly.dev/) and undergoing scheduled maintenance upgrades.
